We stayed in Old historic Istanbul right by the Blue Mosque (Hotel Almira - highly recommend!). Lots of things to see at a walking distance. We hired a guide through viator (Ahmet - GuidedIstanbul Tours). It was a walking tour - we visited Blue Mosque, Hippodrome, Hagia Sophia, Underground Cistern, Grand bazaar, Spice bazaar and then took a tram to Dolmabache palace. All this in one day (9 am - 5 pm). There weren't many tourists and no lines due to recent shooting etc. Our guide was very knowledgeable. We were a little worried to go to Istanbul, but we felt safe when we were there. The locals were friendly. Loved the city. Lot of history and culture.

My husband and I decided to kick off our four country vacation in London. We decided to go in Sept. because of the weather and its in the off season. This turned out great for us. The weather was sweat! Not to hot not so cold. The wind did blow a bit. We spent a week in London and still did not get to do every thing we wanted to do. I would suggest to any one what is thinking of going to buy a London City and museum Pass. We saved all kinds of money having the combination. Its nice not to have to worry about change for the Tube or making change for the museums. They also give you a lot of extras. We were not able to do all the extras. I would defiantly do the river cruse! The people are very helpful when it comes to tourist. We had been planing this vacation for a little over a year. The best guide book that we used was sweet.

Paris is just such a wonderful city, lots to see and do and great places to eat. The architecture is something else and a trip to the louvre is a must. The eiffel tower was great to do but be prepared for the crowds, we were ONLY 4 hours in total in line waiting to get to the top, but its just one of those things you have to do! The streets and the atmosphere in Paris is something that is so romantic, of course you must bring your other half with you otherwise I guess it wouldnt feel the same, but We both loved it and cant wait to return again.......... We did the open tour bus ride around the city, its a great way to see the sights, if you are going to drive do so at your peril!! its a crazy city if you drive, i dont know how they do it!
